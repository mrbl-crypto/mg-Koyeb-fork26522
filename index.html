Our Mission / 我们的使命

Why environmental protection matters to everyone / 为什么环境保护关乎每个人

1. Climate Action / 气候行动

Global temperatures are rising, leading to extreme weather. We must reduce carbon emissions to stabilize our climate.

全球气温正在上升，导致极端天气频发。我们必须减少碳排放以稳定气候。

2. Biodiversity / Biodiversity / 生物多样性

Millions of species are facing extinction. Protecting ecosystems ensures the balance of life on Earth.

数以百万计的物种正面临灭绝。保护生态系统能确保地球生命的平衡。

3. Zero Waste / 零废物

Plastic pollution suffocates our oceans and landfills. Shifting to a circular economy is no longer optional.

塑料污染正使我们的海洋和填埋场窒息。转向循环经济已不再是一个选项，而是必然。

Every Action Counts / 每一份行动都很重要

Simple daily habits that can make a big difference / 改变世界的小小日常习惯

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

减少、复用、回收

Minimize single-use plastics, choose reusable items, and separate waste correctly to give materials a second life.

减少使用一次性塑料，选择可重复使用的物品，并正确分类垃圾以赋予材料第二次生命。

Save Energy & Water

节约能源与水资源

Turn off lights when leaving a room, fix leaks, and choose energy-efficient appliances or public transport.

离开房间时关灯，修复漏水，选择节能电器或公共交通出行。

Plant & Protect

种植与保护

Support reforestation, grow local plants in your garden, and protect green spaces in your community.

支持植树造林，在花园中种植本地植物，并保护社区内的绿色空间。
